Description:
	Computer the world coordinates of angles when given a constraint and endeffectors. Uses inverse kinematics to find the angles of the joints that will put the endeffector as close to the constraint. 

Files Include:
      Joint.cpp & Joint.h
      inverseKinematics.cpp
      slVector.H & slVector.cpp
      slMatrix.H & slMatrix.cpp
      slRotation.H & slRotation.cpp
      Makefile

Compilation:
	make
	./inverseKinematics <ik-a.con, ik-b.com, etc.....>
